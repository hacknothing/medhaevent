Instructions

Check Your Robot: Ensure your robot is fully functional, charged, and ready for the race. Perform a last-minute systems check to avoid technical issues during the race.

Know the Track: Familiarize yourself with the race track, including any obstacles and checkpoints. Plan your strategy to navigate the course efficiently.

Follow the Rules: Adhere to all competition rules and guidelines. This includes staying within the designated track boundaries and avoiding any unsportsmanlike conduct.

Safety First: Ensure that your robot operates safely and does not pose a hazard to other participants or spectators. Be mindful of emergency stop procedures if your robot malfunctions.

Communicate Clearly: Maintain clear communication with your team members. Assign specific roles and responsibilities to ensure smooth coordination and quick problem-solving during the race.
