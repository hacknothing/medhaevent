Instructions

Prepare Your Team: Assemble a diverse team with complementary skills. Ensure everyone understands their roles and responsibilities before the hackathon begins.

Understand the Problem Statement: Carefully read and analyze the problem statement provided by the organizers. Make sure you fully understand the requirements and constraints.

Plan and Prioritize: Create a project plan outlining your approach, milestones, and timelines. Prioritize key features and functionalities to ensure you deliver a working prototype within the given timeframe.

Utilize Resources: Take advantage of any resources provided by the organizers, such as APIs, datasets, and mentoring sessions. Use these to enhance your project and address any challenges you encounter.

Focus on Presentation: Prepare a compelling presentation to showcase your project. Highlight the problem, your solution, key features, and the impact of your work. Practice your pitch to ensure clear and confident delivery.
